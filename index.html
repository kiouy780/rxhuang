Short Bio

My name is HUANG, Ruixi. I was born and grew up in Nanchang City, Jiangxi Province, China.

I am a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Science and Technology for Brain-inspired Intelligence (ISTBI), Fudan University. My research interest lies in various fields, including brain science, computational systematic biology and complex system and their applications in medicine.

In June 2022, I obtained B.Sc. degree in Mathematics at the School of Mathematics, Hunan University. My undergraduate advisor was Professor Frank J. Xu.
